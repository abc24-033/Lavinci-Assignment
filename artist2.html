GRIM$

Is a Botswana-born multimedia artist whose work blends digital art, collage, sound and sculpture to explore emotions that words often fail to express.

His style features obscure figures and surreal imagery, creating a raw, immersive experience.

GRIM$ deaws from Afrofutism and underground aesthetics to reflect deep personal and cultural narratives.
