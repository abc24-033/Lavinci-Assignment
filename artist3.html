Gisho Mwangi

Hes a Kenyan contemporary collage artist whose work explores themes of spirituality, politics and media propaganda.

Working both digitally and physically, he blends archival imagery, symbolic elements and bold visual contrasts to challenge perception and provoke dialogue.

His collages reflect a critical view of power, belief systems and the manipulation of truth in morden society.
