Jack Buddah

This artist works at the intersection of abstract expressionism and realism, creating pieces that are both emotionally evocative and visually grounded. Their work is distinguished by a masterful use of acrylic, gouache, and oil pastel, which allows for rich textural contrasts and vibrant layering.

The use of mixed media enables the artist to play with both the fluidity and opacity of different materials, resulting in dynamic compositions that feel both spontaneous and deliberate.

Their art invites viewers into a space where logic and emotion coexist, encouraging personal reflection and emotional resonance.

Whether through vibrant color fields, suggestive forms, or tactile brushwork, the artist is continually exploring the boundary between what is seen and what is felt.
