Ava Moreau

is a neo-expressionist painter based in Paris, France. Her work channels intense emotion through bold color, textured surfaces, and expressive brushstrokes.

Drawing from early expressionism but with a raw, modern edge, Ava’s style evokes deep feeling and personal reflection.

She’s often compared to artists like Jean-Michel Basquiat or Anselm Kiefer, yet her voice remains uniquely Parisian and contemporary.
